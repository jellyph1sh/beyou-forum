Our cookies's policy

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file and covers all types of tracers "deposited and read, for example, when consulting a website, reading an e-mail, installing or using software or a mobile application, regardless of the type of terminal used". Cookies may be placed by the server of the Site visited or by a third-party server (advertising agency, web analytics service, etc.). Cookies do not compromise site security.

Cookies set by Bword Studio

When you connect to our Site, we may install various cookies on your terminal.

The cookies we issue enable us to :

  • Maintaining the smooth running of the Site
  • Manage the acceptance and retention period of cookies
  • Control whether the user is connected or not
  • Determine the type of user (Admin,guest...)

Cookie management

When you visit our Site for the first time, a cookie banner will be displayed informing you that cookies have been set.
